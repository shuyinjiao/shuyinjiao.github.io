Current Courses Taught at NCSU

  • E 115 Introduction to Computing Environments
    E 115 is an introductory course that is mandatory for all incoming College of Engineering students at NCSU. This hybrid format course is designed to equip students with the essential skills and knowledge required to effectively use the computing and technology resources available in the College of Engineering. Throughout this course, students will be introduced to a range of important topics, including campus-based computing resources and applications, such as Linux systems and Google tools. They will also learn about ethics and professionalism in the use of computing resources, as well as receive an introduction to web development and other campus resources. Typically, the course has an enrollment of around 800 students during the Fall semester, with enrollment ranging from 400-600 students during the Spring semester.
