Current Courses Taught at NCSU

  • E 115 Introduction to Computing Environments

    E 115 is a mandatory introductory course for all incoming College of Engineering students at NCSU. This hybrid format equips students with essential skills and knowledge required to effectively use the computing and technology resources available in the College of Engineering. The course covers topics such as accessing and using campus-based computing resources and applications like campus clusters in Linux and Google tools. Students will also learn about ethics, professionalism in the use of computing resources, and get an introduction to web development and other campus resources. Typical enrollment is 800 students per section in the Fall and 400-600 students in the Spring.
